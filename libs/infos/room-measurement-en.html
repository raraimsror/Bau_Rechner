How to Measure Your Room Correctly

Basic Measurements:

  • X (length) – measure along the longest wall
  • Y (width) – perpendicular to the length
  • Z (height) – from floor to ceiling

Non-Standard Walls:

  • Windows and doors – measure the total wall area, the calculator will automatically subtract openings
  • Niches and protrusions – add their area to the total wall area
  • Sloped ceilings – use the average height

Tip: Measure in centimeters for accuracy. Round up by 5-10 cm for material reserve.
